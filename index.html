About Sport Support

Building opportunity through athletics.

Sport Support exists to make sports more accessible for youth who may not otherwise have the financial means to participate. We believe athletics can teach discipline, confidence, teamwork, and resilience — and every child deserves the chance to benefit from those experiences.

Based in Dallas, TX, we work to connect community generosity with real, practical support for young athletes and their families. Through fundraising events like local 5Ks, donor contributions, and community partnerships, we help cover athletic-related expenses for youth in need.

Our Vision

A community where every child has a fair opportunity to play, compete, and grow through sports.

Our Focus

Direct support for youth athletic participation, with a focus on accessibility, encouragement, and local impact.

Community Impact

Every donation and every event entry helps create more opportunities for youth in need to join teams, stay active, and feel supported in their athletic journey.

Need help with youth athletic expenses?
